When you first open Securo after creating your user account, the app guides you through initial configuration. This ensures you have everything you need to start tracking your finances.

## Step 1: Set Your Primary Currency

Choose the currency you use most. This becomes the **primary currency** for your dashboard, budgets, and reports. Securo supports many world currencies including BRL, USD, EUR, GBP, and more.

If you manage money in multiple currencies, don't worry, you can create accounts in different currencies later, and Securo will convert everything to your primary currency for aggregated views.

## Step 2: Add Your First Account

Create at least one financial account to start tracking. You'll need to provide:

- **Account name**: Something descriptive like "Checking Account" or "Nubank"
- **Account type**: Checking, Savings, Credit Card, Cash, etc.
- **Currency**: The account's currency
- **Starting balance**: How much is currently in the account

The starting balance is your account's value right now. All future transactions will be calculated relative to this number, so try to enter the exact current balance.

## Step 3: Import Transactions (Optional)

If you have transaction history from your bank, you can import it immediately. Securo accepts **OFX, CSV, QIF, and CAMT** files. See the [Import guide](/docs/features/import) for details on each format.

You can skip this step and import later, or just start fresh and track going forward.

## Step 4: Set Up Categories

Securo comes with default [categories](/docs/features/categories) that cover common spending areas. Review them and customize as needed: add categories specific to your life, rename the defaults, or reorganize the groups.

## After the Wizard

Once you've completed setup, you'll land on the [Dashboard](/docs/features/dashboard). From here, we recommend:

1. **Add a few transactions** manually to get familiar with the flow
2. **Create categorization rules** in [Rules](/docs/features/rules) so future transactions are sorted automatically
3. **Set up budgets** in [Budgets](/docs/features/budgets) for your main spending categories
4. **Add recurring transactions** in [Recurring](/docs/features/recurring) for subscriptions and regular bills
