Goals help you save toward something specific — an emergency fund, a trip, a down payment — by setting a target and tracking your progress over time.

![Goals](/screenshots/goals.png)

## Creating a Goal

Go to **Goals** in the sidebar and click **Add Goal**. Fill in:

1. **Name**: What you're saving for
2. **Target Amount**: How much you want to reach
3. **Currency**: The goal's currency
4. **Target Date** (optional): When you want to reach it
5. **Tracking Type**: How progress is measured (see below)
6. **Icon & Color**: A visual identity for the goal

## Tracking Types

The **tracking type** determines how Securo knows how much you've saved:

| Type | How progress is measured |
|------|--------------------------|
| **Manual** | You enter the current amount yourself and update it as you go |
| **Linked Account** | Progress follows the balance of a specific account |
| **Linked Asset** | Progress follows the value of a specific asset |
| **Net Worth** | Progress follows your total net worth |

Linked tracking keeps your goals up to date automatically — as the account, asset, or net worth changes, the goal's progress updates with it.

## Progress and Status

Each goal shows a progress bar and a percentage toward its target. When a goal has a target date, Securo also shows an **on-track** status so you know whether you're keeping pace:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Ahead** | You're saving faster than needed to hit the target on time |
| **On Track** | You're on pace to reach the target by the target date |
| **Behind** | You need to save faster to reach the target on time |
| **Overdue** | The target date has passed and the goal isn't complete |
| **Achieved** | The goal has been completed |

## Managing Goals

Each goal has actions for its lifecycle:

- **Pause / Resume**: Temporarily stop tracking a goal, then pick it back up
- **Mark Complete**: Mark a goal as achieved
- **Archive**: Move a finished or abandoned goal out of the active list
- **Edit / Delete**: Change a goal's details or remove it

Use the **Active**, **Completed**, **Paused**, **Archived**, and **All** filters at the top of the page to switch between them.

## Goals on the Dashboard

Active goals also appear in a **Goals Progress** widget on the [Dashboard](/docs/features/dashboard), with a compact progress bar and on-track status for each — so you see where you stand every time you open Securo.
