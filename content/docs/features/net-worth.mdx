The Net Worth page gives you a high-level view of your total financial position: all accounts and assets combined, tracked over time.

![Net Worth](/screenshots/net-worth.png)

## Overview Card

At the top, a summary card shows:

- **Net Worth**: Your total net worth in your primary currency
- **Percentage change**: How much your net worth has grown (or shrunk) compared to the start of the selected period
- **Absolute change**: The actual amount gained or lost
- **Accounts total**: Combined balance of all bank/cash accounts
- **Assets total**: Combined value of all tracked assets (investments, property, etc.)

## Net Worth Over Time

A line chart tracks your net worth month by month. The chart shows three series:

- **Accounts**: Your liquid money in bank accounts and wallets
- **Assets**: Investments, property, and other assets
- **Liabilities**: Debts and credit card balances

Hover over any point to see the exact values for that month.

## Composition View

Below the chart, a **Composition** section breaks down what makes up your net worth:

- **Summary view**: Shows the total net worth as a single number
- **Detailed view**: Lists every account and asset with its individual balance and contribution to the total

Toggle between Summary and Detailed using the buttons in the top right of the composition card.

## Time Range

Like other reports, you can adjust the time range:

- **6M**: Last 6 months
- **1Y**: Last 1 year (default)
- **2Y**: Last 2 years

## Multi-Currency Totals

If you have accounts in different currencies, Securo converts everything to your primary currency for the net worth calculation. Individual accounts still show their native currency amounts alongside the converted values.
