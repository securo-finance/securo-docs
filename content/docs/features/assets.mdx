The Assets page is where you track everything you own that isn't sitting in a bank account — stocks, ETFs, crypto, real estate, vehicles, and other valuables. Each asset's value feeds into your overall [Net Worth](/docs/features/net-worth).

![Assets](/screenshots/assets.png)

## Asset Types

Securo supports a range of asset types, each with its own icon:

| Type | Examples |
|------|----------|
| **Stock** | Individual company shares |
| **ETF** | Exchange-traded funds |
| **Crypto** | Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other tokens |
| **Fund** | Mutual funds and managed funds |
| **Real Estate** | Houses, apartments, land |
| **Vehicle** | Cars, motorcycles, boats |
| **Valuable** | Jewelry, art, collectibles |
| **Investment** | Generic investments |
| **Other** | Anything else |

## Wallets

Assets are organized into **wallets** — groups like "Retirement", "Trading", or "Crypto". Each wallet shows a combined total and can be expanded or collapsed. Assets that aren't assigned to a wallet appear in an **Ungrouped** section.

To create a wallet, click **+ New Wallet**. You can also create one inline while adding an asset.

## Adding an Asset

Click **+ Add Asset** and fill in the asset's **name**, **type**, **currency**, and optionally a **wallet**. You can also record a **purchase date** and **purchase price** so Securo can calculate your gain or loss.

The most important choice is the **valuation method**, which determines how the asset's value is kept up to date. It's locked once the asset is created.

### Valuation Methods

| Method | How it works |
|--------|--------------|
| **Manual** | You enter the value yourself and update it whenever you like. Best for property, vehicles, and valuables. |
| **Growth Rule** | You set a growth rate (percentage or fixed amount) and frequency (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly), and Securo projects the value automatically from a start date. |
| **Market Price** | You enter a **ticker** (e.g., `AAPL`, `BTC-USD`, `PETR4.SA`) and a **quantity**. Securo fetches the live price and keeps the value current. Best for stocks, ETFs, and crypto. |

For market-price assets, Securo shows a live quote preview as you type the ticker, and refreshes prices automatically each day.

## Value History

Every asset keeps a **value history**. For manual assets, use the **+ Add Value** form to record a new value with a date. Each entry shows the change from the previous one, color-coded green for gains and red for losses. Once an asset has two or more values, a trend chart appears in its detail panel.

Growth-rule and market-price assets build their history automatically.

## Synced Assets

If you connect a brokerage or investment account through [Bank Sync](/docs/features/bank-sync), your holdings are imported automatically as assets. Synced assets:

- Are marked with a **Synced** badge and grouped into a wallet named after the institution
- Update their values automatically on each sync
- Are read-only — they're managed by the connection, so editing and deleting are disabled

You can still rename a synced wallet and move synced assets between wallets.

## Portfolio Value

At the top of the page, the **Portfolio Value** chart shows your total asset value over time as a stacked area chart. Toggle between **By Wallet** and **By Asset** to change how the value is broken down.

## Assets and Net Worth

The combined value of all your assets is converted to your primary currency and added to your [Net Worth](/docs/features/net-worth), alongside your account balances. Selling an asset — by recording a **sell date** and **sell price** — moves it into a **Sold Assets** section while keeping its history for reporting.
