The reports section provides visual analytics to help you understand your financial patterns over time.

![Reports](/screenshots/reports.png)

## Income vs Expenses

The **Income vs Expenses** report shows a side-by-side comparison of money coming in versus money going out, broken down by month.

This report helps you answer the fundamental question: am I spending more than I earn? The visualization makes it easy to spot months where expenses exceeded income.

## Time Range Controls

All reports support flexible time ranges:

- **6M**: Last 6 months
- **1Y**: Last 1 year
- **2Y**: Last 2 years

You can also toggle between different granularity levels:

- **D**: Daily
- **S**: Weekly
- **M**: Monthly
- **A**: Yearly

## Cash Flow

The **Cash Flow** report tracks the net money movement over time: income minus expenses per period. A positive cash flow means you're saving; a negative one means you're spending more than you earn.

> This feature is marked as "coming soon" (em breve) in the current version.

## How to Use Reports

1. Navigate to **Reports** in the sidebar
2. Select the report type using the tabs at the top
3. Adjust the time range and granularity using the controls
4. Hover over chart elements for detailed tooltips

Reports are read-only and always reflect your current transaction data. There's nothing to configure, they update automatically as you add and categorize transactions.
