The dashboard is the first screen you see after logging in. It provides a monthly overview of your financial health, with widgets that update as you add and categorize transactions.

![Dashboard](/screenshots/dashboard.png)

## Monthly Overview

At the top of the dashboard you'll see the current month and navigation arrows to browse previous months. The main summary card displays:

- **Savings Rate**: The percentage of income you saved this month
- **Total Balance**: The combined balance across all your accounts, shown in your primary currency
- **Monthly Income**: Total money received this month
- **Monthly Expenses**: Total money spent this month
- **Net Worth**: Your total net worth including assets

Below the summary, the dashboard shows a spending forecast: based on your current pace, it estimates how much you'll spend by the end of the month.

## Uncategorized Transactions

If you have transactions that haven't been assigned a category yet, a prominent badge appears showing the count and total amount. Clicking **Categorize now** takes you directly to the transaction list filtered to uncategorized items so you can quickly sort them.

This is important because uncategorized transactions won't appear correctly in your budget tracking or spending reports.

## Spending by Category

This widget shows a horizontal bar chart of your top spending categories for the month. Each bar displays:

- The category name and icon
- The amount spent
- A percentage change compared to the previous month
- A progress bar showing spending relative to your budget (if one is set)

This gives you an instant view of where your money is going without navigating to the full reports section.

## Balance Flow

The balance flow chart shows how your total balance changed day-by-day throughout the month. It's a line chart that helps you spot:

- Large one-time expenses (sudden drops)
- Income deposits (sharp increases)
- Overall spending trends across the month

Hover over any point to see the exact balance for that day.

## Period Transactions

At the bottom, you'll find a list of the most recent transactions for the selected month. Each entry shows the description, date, category, and amount. Click any transaction to view its full details or edit it.

## Account Balances

The sidebar displays your accounts with their current balances. Accounts in foreign currencies show both the original currency amount and the converted value in your primary currency. A percentage change indicator shows how each account balance has changed recently.
