Budgets help you control spending by setting a monthly limit for each category. As you spend, Securo tracks how much of your budget is used and how much remains, giving you a clear view of whether you're on track.

![Budgets](/screenshots/budgets.png)

## How Budgets Work

A budget in Securo is simple: you pick a category and set a maximum amount you want to spend on it per month. Securo then compares your actual spending in that category against the limit.

For example, if you budget R$500 for Groceries and you've spent R$320 so far this month, the budget view shows you have R$180 remaining.

## Creating a Budget

1. Navigate to **Budgets** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ Add Budget**
3. Select a category from the dropdown
4. Enter the monthly limit amount
5. Click **Save**

You can create budgets for as many categories as you like. Each budget operates independently.

## Monthly View

The budgets page shows the current month by default, with navigation arrows to browse past months. For each budget you'll see:

- The **category** name and icon
- The **budgeted** amount (your limit)
- The **spent** amount so far
- The **remaining** amount
- A **progress bar** showing how close you are to the limit

When you exceed a budget, the progress bar and remaining amount turn red to alert you.

## Tips for Effective Budgeting

- **Start with your biggest categories**: Budget for the areas where you spend the most first (housing, food, transportation)
- **Review monthly**: At the end of each month, check which budgets were too tight or too loose and adjust
- **Use with rules**: Set up [categorization rules](/docs/features/rules) so transactions are automatically categorized, keeping your budget tracking accurate without manual work
- **Check the dashboard**: The dashboard's spending-by-category widget gives you a quick budget health check without navigating to the budgets page
