We believe personal finance should actually be _personal_. No corporation should sit between you and your financial data. Securo runs on your own infrastructure, giving you full visibility into your accounts, spending, and habits — without surrendering a single byte to third parties.

## Features

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="Multi-Account Management" icon="laptop">
		Track multiple accounts with real-time running balances.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Transaction Management" icon="list-format">
		Search, filter, and export transactions to CSV.
	</Card>
	<Card title="File Import" icon="document">
		Import OFX, QIF, CAMT, and CSV files from your bank.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Auto-Categorization" icon="puzzle">
		Rules engine to automatically categorize transactions.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Budgets & Recurring" icon="approve-check">
		Set budgets and track recurring transactions.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Bank Sync" icon="external">
		Sync with your bank via the Pluggy integration.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>
